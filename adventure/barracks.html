Your Evidence

Congratulations! You found another secret room! Go there to get an even more beneficial clue!

To go to the secret room, you'll have to pass seven trials. They are quick timed events involving the range slider.

The slider is numbered from 1 to 7 (but you won't see those numbers). Each trial will give you a number, you have to slide the slider to that number. (Press the 'enter' key instead of 'submit' after you've slid the slider to the given number.)

Complete all seven trials successfully within the time limit, which is 8 seconds, and you'll be granted the identity of the outsider!
